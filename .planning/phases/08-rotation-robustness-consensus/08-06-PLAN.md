<objective>
Two fail-closed build/chain hardening fixes. ROTATE-13 (D-01): `buildRefounding`'s compaction loop today silently `continue`s a non-plaintext head and swallows a `rewrapSeal` throw, so it can ship a partial `compactionWraps` — convert to detect-and-abort (throw before any publish). ROTATE-12 (L01): `buildChain` spreads the tip's `refounder` onto every historical epoch, and `held_roots` entries have no `refounder` field to attribute correctly — a forged-roster vector the moment any per-epoch fold surfaces.

Purpose: A Refounding that cannot reliably fold the whole Control Plane aborts rather than shipping a partial compaction; historical epoch material is attributed to the npub whose Refounding actually minted it.
Output: Abort-on-unfoldable-head in buildRefounding; per-epoch refounder attribution across types.ts/rollForward/buildChain.
</objective>
