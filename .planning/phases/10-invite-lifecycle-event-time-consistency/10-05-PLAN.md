<objective>
Make a revoked invite link unjoinable even when a lagging relay still serves the old bundle (INVITE-01/D-01/D-02/D-03): resolve the coordinate `(33301, link_signer, "")` to its newest event FIRST, then evaluate the tombstone on that single winner. The current `joinByLink` filters revoked bundles OUT then picks the newest survivor — the exact inversion CORD-05 §2 forbids, where a `vsk 9` tombstone wins only when it is the sole returned event.

Purpose: A tombstone is "exactly as durable as the bundle it replaced" (CORD-05 §2) and must compete for "newest" on equal footing, never be pre-excluded.
Output: An inline `newestAtCoordinate` collapse in `joinByLink`, a scoped `"#d": [""]` request filter, and a lagging-relay repro test.
</objective>
