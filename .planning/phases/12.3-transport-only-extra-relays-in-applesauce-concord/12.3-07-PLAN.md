<objective>
Prove the reactive and arithmetic half of the phase, which the canary suite cannot
see: that a later extras emission actually reaches live sockets and status
observables (D-08/D-09/D-11), that a no-op emission does not churn them (D-09,
RESEARCH Pitfall 4), that extras participate in stream-key AUTH (D-03), and that
refounding's quorum did not move (D-06 / ROTATE-09).

These are the failure modes that ship silently. A first-value-only resolver at a
long-lived site produces no error and no visible symptom until an app updates its
extras and nothing happens; a widened quorum denominator produces no error until a
refounding becomes impossible. Each gets a test that fails loudly.

Runs in wave 5 alongside plan 06 — different files, no overlap.

Purpose: enforce D-11's continuous-versus-one-shot split and pin the Phase 8
arithmetic.
Output: new test cases in the three existing engine suites.

No changeset (D-15).
</objective>
