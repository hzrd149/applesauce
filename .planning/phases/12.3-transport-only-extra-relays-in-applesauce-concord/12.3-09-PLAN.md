<objective>
Close the D-06/ROTATE-09 verification gap: `refound()`'s majority arithmetic is
computed from two differently-normalized views of the same relay list, and the
normalization step itself throws on any entry `new URL()` rejects. Both defects
are new in this phase's diff and both are reachable from an invite bundle,
whose `relays` array is never validated beyond `Array.isArray` plus a length
cap.

Purpose: Refounding is the CORD-06 ban/exclusion mechanism. A hostile or merely
malformed invite link currently either crashes it with an opaque parse error or
makes its quorum permanently unsatisfiable — a denial of a security-critical
operation from untrusted content.

Output: one derived protocol relay set feeding both halves of the quorum
arithmetic, tolerant ack attribution, hardened bundle relay validation, and
regression tests that fail against the current implementation.
</objective>
