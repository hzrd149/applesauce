<objective>
Close the three Grant-fold authority holes in `foldControl` (M05/M06/S01) inside the shared read-path reducer `control.ts`, each landing with its own hand-derived spec-value test and a non-vacuity check. All three edit the single Grant fold loop (`control.ts:174-198`); they are folded into one plan because they are one coherent rewrite of that loop.

Purpose: A forged Grant at a bogus coordinate, a malformed `role_ids` that DoS-es every member's fold, and a junior stripping a senior via a vacuous `[].every()` revoke are all live privilege/availability holes today. This plan makes the read-path Grant fold coordinate-bound, total (never throws), and strictly-outrank-gated on its target — per locked decisions D-01, D-02, D-07, D-08, D-10.

Output: hardened Grant fold in `control.ts` + three spec-derived tests in `control.test.ts`.
</objective>
