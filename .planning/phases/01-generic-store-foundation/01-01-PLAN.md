<objective>
Introduce the NIP-59 rumor verifier and genericize the CORE-04 structural helpers over `E extends StoreEvent = NostrEvent`, with zero behavior change for signed `NostrEvent` callers.

Purpose: This is the foundation layer of the Phase 1 type migration. Managers (Plan 03) and the store classes (Plan 04) call these helpers with `E`-typed arguments, so the helpers must be generic first for those later plans to type-check. `verifyRumor` (RUMOR-02) is the verifier `RumorStore` will adopt in Phase 3; it is introduced and unit-tested here (D-03) so success criterion #3 is proven where the verifier lives.

Output: A generic structural-helper surface, an exported `verifyRumor`, a focused verifyRumor unit test, and an updated exports snapshot — the build and full core test suite stay green.
</objective>
