**Migrated all six SealSymbol/RumorSymbol/GiftWrapSymbol accumulated-state writes in `applesauce-common`'s `gift-wrap.ts` from `Reflect.set` to non-enumerable `setCachedValue`, with regression tests proving Set-mutation and sentinel semantics survive.**

## Accomplishments
- `addParentSealReference` (:55) and `getRumorSeals` (:95) Set-init writes now non-enumerable via `setCachedValue`, while the returned `Set` keeps a `writable: true` descriptor so later `.add()` calls on the same reference still work exactly as before
- `getSealRumor`'s failure sentinel (:162, `undefined`) and success write (:183) migrated mechanically to `setCachedValue`; `isSealUnlocked`'s known presence-check gap (WR-10) was explicitly left untouched, per plan scope
- `getGiftWrapSeal`'s `GiftWrapSymbol` (:217) and `SealSymbol` (:222) cross-reference writes migrated to `setCachedValue`, completing all 6 Group A gift-wrap sites in `applesauce-common` from 05.1-PATTERNS.md's inventory
