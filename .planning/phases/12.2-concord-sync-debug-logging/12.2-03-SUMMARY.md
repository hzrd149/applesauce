- Followed the mandatory derive-once logging convention from the wave-1 code-review finding: the module-level `log` in `relay-auth.ts` is derived exactly once and called directly at every site; no `.extend()` call appears anywhere except the single module-scope declaration and the two files' existing one-time constructor-time `this.log = options.logger ?? logger.extend("invite")` assignments (which predate this plan and are exempt per the convention's "acceptable" carve-out).
