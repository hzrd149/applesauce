# Phase 12.1 Plan 01: Concord Sync Skips Ephemeral Kind 21059 Summary

**Removed the dead ephemeral-21059 half of Concord's `syncAuthors` backfill filter behind a named `BACKFILL_KINDS` constant, leaving the two live-subscription sites unchanged, and proved the fix with a non-vacuous two-directional filter-spy test.**

## Accomplishments

- `syncAuthors` (the single backfill entry point shared by the core-plane and channel walks) now requests only kind 1059, never wasting a round-trip on ephemeral 21059, which relays never retain
- `BACKFILL_KINDS` is a new exported, documented constant in `gift-wrap.ts` that structurally guards against silently re-adding 21059 to the backfill walk
- The two live-subscription sites (`community.ts:619`, `private-channel.ts:256`) are untouched and still request both kinds — the backfill/live asymmetry is now explicit in the code, not incidental
- A two-directional filter-spy regression test locks in both halves of the asymmetry, and the backfill assertion was empirically proven non-vacuous against the pre-fix filter
